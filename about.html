The prototype *Test My Internet* page keeps an eye on your internet connection. It displays a list of hosts that it can detect (Google, and maybe your router), and then continually checks to see if they're working. Success/failure information is displayed in the text on the right. Leave the page open while you work on other things (perhaps in another window). My requests for testers: - Let it run for a while (hours, or a day) - Tell me the list of hosts it finds (on the left). There will be one or two in this prototype. - Did it detect any outages? Do they correspond with any interruptions you saw? - Let me know what it shows in the text on the right (copy/paste would be fine) - Tell me the browser (Chrome, IE, Edge, Firefox, etc.) and OS (Windows, Mac, other) that you're using - Let me know your reaction to what it provides, and features you'd like to have added - And of course, please tell me of bugs/weirdness that you notice Thanks again.
